About me

My name is Marc Anguera Insa, born in 1986. I spent the last years in Barcelona studying, developing and sharing good moments with a lot of people.

During these years I achieved the computer engineering career in Barcelona School of Informatics (FIB) faculty part of Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC).

Currently I'm working on the analysis and development of web applications. Especially using Ruby and some of its frameworks (like Ruby on Rails) and Javascript.

I like to participate in the different areas of a project and build comfortable and friendly applications for the users and the internet. I always try to find pragmatic and simple solutions. And follow good practices, both development and organization side.
